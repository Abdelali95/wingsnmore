About Us

A local favorite for comfort food done right

At Wings N More, we serve up bold flavor, fresh ingredients, and a menu packed with something for everyone. From crispy buffalo wings and hearty burgers to seafood platters, gyros, tenders, salads, and sandwiches, every order is made to satisfy.

Known as a dependable local favorite, we take pride in family-friendly service, generous portions, and food that keeps guests coming back. Whether you're grabbing lunch, dinner, or a pickup order for the family, Wings N More delivers flavor you can count on.
